FAQ

1. Is it possible to make good money on Airdrops?

A. Yes, many times we could see projects that gave away to their community considerable amounts of hundreds, thousands, or tens of thousands of $ For example, such projects as:

Arbitrum (the average amount of tokens brought was 2.5 thousand, you could sell it quietly on the exchange at a price of $ 1.3 per piece, so the profit in this case was 3250$)

Sui ($ 2250 if someone drew allocations)

Uniswap (if someone was patient and sold his tokens in a bull market, he could make $20k+)

2. Is it a sure profit?

A. It is not a certain gain. Acting in terms of Airdrops in the vast majority of cases we speculate that such an airdrop will occur, they appear frequently, but it is nothing certain. Through our activities, analyses we try to get as much information as possible that will bring us closer to getting an Airdrop.

3. Will I get an airdrop after a month already?

A. Most likely not, projects usually do not rush to release their token. You need to act consistently from the beginning and set yourself up for a couple of months. This is not a sprint, but a marathon. ðŸ’ª
